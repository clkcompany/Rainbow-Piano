Privacy Policy Rainbow Piano – Privacy Policy Last updated: May 1, 2025 1. Overview Rainbow Piano ("the App") is a music education app designed for children and families. We are committed to protecting your privacy. 2. Information We Collect We do NOT collect any personal information. We do NOT require account registration. We do NOT track your location. We do NOT use advertising networks. We do NOT share data with third parties. All progress data (stars, scores, practice streaks) is stored locally on your device only and never transmitted anywhere. 3. In-App Purchases The App offers a one-time "Pro" upgrade via Google Play / Apple App Store billing. We do not process or store any payment information — all billing is handled entirely by Google or Apple. 4. Notifications The App may send local practice reminder notifications. These are generated on your device only. You can disable them at any time in your device settings. 5. Children's Privacy (COPPA) Rainbow Piano is designed for children aged 3 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, the app is fully COPPA compliant. 6. Third-Party Services The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. 7. Data Security Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no data at risk. 8. Changes to This Policy If we update this policy, the new version will be posted at the same URL with an updated date. 9. Contact For privacy questions:tech@clk.company
