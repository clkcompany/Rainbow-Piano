Overview

Rainbow Piano is a music education app designed for children and families. We are fully committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we handle and how.

Information We Collect

  • We do NOT collect any personal information
  • We do NOT require account registration
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT use advertising networks
  • We do NOT share any data with third parties

All progress data (stars, scores, practice streaks) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted anywhere.

In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time "Pro" upgrade via Google Play or Apple App Store billing. We do not process or store any payment information — all billing is handled entirely by Google or Apple under their own privacy policies.

Notifications

The App may send local practice reminder notifications. These are generated entirely on your device — no data is sent to any server. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Rainbow Piano is designed for children aged 3 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we collect absolutely no data, the App is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting tools, or tracking libraries. What happens on your device stays on your device.

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no personal data at risk. App preferences and progress are stored using the standard secure local storage provided by your operating system.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated date. We encourage you to review it periodically.
